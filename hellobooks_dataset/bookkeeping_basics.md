# Bookkeeping Basics

Bookkeeping is the process of recording and organizing financial transactions of a business. It ensures that all financial activities such as sales, purchases, payments, and receipts are properly documented.

## Key Responsibilities
- Recording daily financial transactions
- Maintaining ledgers
- Tracking accounts payable and receivable
- Reconciling bank statements
- Preparing financial reports

## Why Bookkeeping Matters
Accurate bookkeeping helps businesses:
- Monitor financial health
- Make informed financial decisions
- Prepare tax reports
- Prevent fraud or financial mismanagement
