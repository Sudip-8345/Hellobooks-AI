# Balance Sheet

A balance sheet provides a snapshot of a company's financial position at a specific point in time.

## Core Equation

Assets = Liabilities + Equity

## Assets
Resources owned by the company such as:
- Cash
- Inventory
- Equipment
- Accounts receivable

## Liabilities
Financial obligations such as:
- Loans
- Accounts payable
- Taxes owed

## Equity
Owner’s share in the business after liabilities are deducted.
