# Profit and Loss Statement (P&L)

A Profit and Loss statement summarizes the revenues, costs, and expenses incurred during a specific period.

## Main Components

Revenue:
Income generated from business activities.

Expenses:
Operational costs such as rent, salaries, and utilities.

Net Profit or Loss:
Net Profit = Revenue - Expenses

If expenses exceed revenue, the result is a net loss.

## Importance
- Measures business profitability
- Helps track performance over time
- Assists in financial planning
