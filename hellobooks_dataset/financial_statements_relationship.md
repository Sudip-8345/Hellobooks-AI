# Relationship Between Financial Statements

The Profit & Loss statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement are interconnected.

## Profit & Loss
Shows profitability over a period.

## Balance Sheet
Shows financial position at a specific time.

## Cash Flow Statement
Shows movement of cash during the period.

Example:
Net profit from the Profit & Loss statement contributes to retained earnings in the Balance Sheet.

Changes in cash appear in the Cash Flow Statement.
